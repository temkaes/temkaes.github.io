Offshore Engineering Services

We are the most experienced offshore container construction and repair company in the Caspian Sea region.

Our company has been particularly successful in delivering new and used DNV 2.7-1 (now DNVGL-ST-E271) certified containers. We have been trusted service partners for major container leasing and trading companies. We also have in-house capability to design new offshore containers, skips, baskets, modules (office, workshop, accommodation) and racks. Having delivered more than 1,200 newly constructed offshore containers and units, our experience is unmatched in this sector.

Other Offshore Services

Manpower & Crewing

We have supplied and managed teams of mechanics, fitters, motorman, electricians, roustabouts, welders, foremen, doctors and casual labour to major operators in the oil & gas industry. Our teams are certified, qualified and fully insured.

Logistics Support

We can provide various logistics services for offshore operators, including transportation (land and marine), storage (closed space with 24/7 security) and procurement of equipment, supplies and materials.

Fabrication & Repair

We can fabricate and repair frames, modules, blocks, decks and other structures both onshore (and then deliver and install offshore) and offshore. We are equally capable with fabrication and repair of piping, accommodation and HVAC systems.

Equipment Supply & Service

We offer service and supply of oil & gas and auxiliary equipment and machineries such as generators, compressors, motors, switchboards, and much more. These services can be realized both offshore and onshore, depending on the application.
