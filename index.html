We are expert fabricators and engineers.

Founded and led by industry veterans with decades of experience, our company specializes in fabrication, repair, blasting & painting, system modifications & upgrades, especially for the offshore and marine industry. We also have a survey division offering hull & machinery surveys for owners and underwriters.

OUR SERVICES

BLASTING & PAINTING


With our experienced and trained crew, we can carry out top-notch blasting and painting jobs to offshore and marine standards. We are experienced with all types of coating technologies used in the civil, offshore and marine industries. We have our own mobile equipment which we can deploy out to different sites if necessary.


OFFSHORE CONTAINERS


We have the most experienced team in fabrication and repair of offshore certified containers, in the region. Our team has been delivering new offshore (ABS and DNV GL) certified containers in the Caspian Sea for more than 2 decades. We also offer full service repair and modification of offshore units. We can build containers, skips, office/accommodation modules, equipment modules and much more.

CIVIL ENGINEERING


Our team has a wide experience in civil engineering jobs also, which has supplemented our marine & offshore projects for decades. From simple sheds and gates to large warehouses, truss structures and platforms - we have the skills, experience and tools to meet all your needs in this field.

HULL & MACHINERY SURVEYS


With our extensive knowledge and experience in the marine and offshore industry, we are one of very few companies in the Caspian region offering hull & machinery surveys for asset owners and insurers. Our in-depth surveys with proper documentation and reporting will bring clarity to all parties involved.
